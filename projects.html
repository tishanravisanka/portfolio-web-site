PlantUp

PlantUp is an automated agricultural system which focuses on greenhouses and any kind of cultivation. Using smart sensors crops can be equally monitored with a reduction of manual labor.
PlantUp Greenhouse system is an IoT controlled environmental system that allows a farmer to make precision adjustments to his environment remotely. This product can sense the temperature, amount of light, CO2 levels, humidity, light and soil moisture readings through the web application and control the environment conditions with a suitable manner using fans, heaters, venture injectors and many other sensors. There is a water pump to maintain the water levels in the soil and to supply the amount of fertilizer needed so that the nutrient levels can be controlled as well. Also, pH level of the water can be measured through the system

FuelMind

This is a system to prevent steeling fuel from the vehicles (Mainly bus drivers fill kerosene instead of diesel to their buses). Our device should be connected to the fuel indicating cable and through a mobile application fuel level and the location can be tracked. When fuel level is increasing, user will be notified with the time and the location. So the user can identify is it a filling station or any other place. As additional features user can track the vehicle route, get the daily mileage and get the parking times.
